🍎 iPhone / iPad Users

The easiest way to use SmartShop on iPhone is through Safari. You can also add it to your Home Screen so it feels much more like a normal app.

  1. Tap Open SmartShop above.
  2. If needed, open the page in Safari.
  3. Tap the Share button in Safari.
  4. Scroll and tap Add to Home Screen.
  5. Tap Add.
  6. Open SmartShop anytime from your Home Screen.
If you opened the page from Facebook or another app, look for Open in Safari first. After that, adding it to your Home Screen is easy.

🤖 Android Users

You can use SmartShop right in your browser, or request the Android version through your SmartShop account if you prefer a more app-like install experience.

  1. For the simplest option, tap Open SmartShop.
  2. Log in and start using it right away.
  3. If you want the Android version, tap Download Android APK.
  4. You may be asked to log in or confirm access before the download starts.
  5. Open the file on your Android phone and follow the install prompts.
  6. Once installed, sign in and your account should sync normally.
Not into downloading apps outside the Play Store? Totally fair — the web app is still the easiest option and works great.
